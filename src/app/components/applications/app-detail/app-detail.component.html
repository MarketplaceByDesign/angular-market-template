About

With this Application you can collaborate with teammates at any time, from any where in the world. Send an email, text message, start a video call or create a new project thread all in real time. All of your conversations will automatically sync across every device.

Team administrators can create public or private threads, add or remove team members and even invite people from external organizations to collaborate on a project.
